Home Furnitures

Bedroom Furniture

We design and craft a wide range of bedroom furniture, including beds, wardrobes, dressers, and nightstands. Our goal is to create pieces that blend functionality with style, ensuring your bedroom is a comfortable and welcoming retreat. We use high-quality materials to build furniture that lasts and enhances the overall look of your bedroom.

Kitchen Cabinets and Units

Our kitchen cabinets and storage units are custom-built to maximize space and improve the functionality of your kitchen. We offer designs that not only look modern but also make everyday tasks easier. From sleek, contemporary styles to classic designs, we create cabinets that suit your taste and fit seamlessly into your kitchen layout.

Hall Furniture

We provide stylish and durable furniture for your living room, including TV units, coffee tables, and sofas. Our designs are crafted to enhance the ambiance of your living space while offering practical solutions for your daily needs. Whether you prefer modern, traditional, or transitional styles, our furniture will make your living room both attractive and comfortable.

Cupboards and Storage

We create practical storage solutions such as wardrobes and cupboards that help keep your home organized. Our designs are tailored to fit your space and storage needs, with features that enhance usability and aesthetic appeal. We aim to provide furniture that makes storage easy and efficient.

Tables and Chairs

We offer a wide selection of tables and chairs suitable for various uses, including dining tables, office desks, and more. Our furniture is built to be sturdy and comfortable, making it perfect for both casual and formal settings. We focus on quality craftsmanship to ensure that each piece is functional and stylish.

Sofas

Our range of sofas includes different styles and sizes to fit any room. We design sofas with comfort and durability in mind, using materials that provide long-lasting support. Whether you need a compact sofa for a small space or a larger sectional for a family room, we have options that will meet your needs.

Fittings And Repair 🔨

At Dharmik Furniture, we offer a full furniture repair and fitting service to keep your furniture in great shape. Our expert team can handle everything from small fixes to big repairs, like broken joints, damaged surfaces, and faulty hardware. We provide professional fitting services for furniture that needs reassembly, adjustment, or updating to work better and look nicer. Whether it's a wobbly table, a broken cabinet door, or any other problem, our skilled technicians use high-quality materials and techniques to fix it. Our goal is to make your furniture last longer and work better. With our repair and fitting services, you can trust that your favorite pieces will be fixed quickly and well, so they can serve you for many more years.

About Us

At Dharmik Furniture, we are committed to delivering exceptional carpentry and furniture solutions with a personal touch. Founded and managed by three brothers—Mahesh, Prakash, and Jiten Mistry—our family-run business has earned a reputation for quality and reliability. Mahesh caters to Khambhat and surrounding areas, Prakash takes care of Ahmedabad, and Jiten serves Anand. Together, we have successfully completed over 300 projects, each reflecting our dedication to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to create beautiful, functional furniture that enhances your living and working spaces while providing outstanding service and support.
